Welcome to Our Salon

Started in 2025, Arambagh Jawed Habib was born from a passion for creating a welcoming space where every family member can feel pampered and stylish. Our team of experienced stylists is dedicated to providing top-notch service and personalized care.

We believe that a great haircut can boost confidence and that a moment of relaxation is essential. We use high-quality products and stay up-to-date with the latest trends to ensure you always leave looking and feeling your best.
